Inspitational websites

Some ideas come from these websites images such as the manchester adoption form which led to the idea of placing one at the end of the webpage. The image backgrounds that take all the space are also interesting and give an impactful and direct message. The navigation menu has interesting hover applications. There is also animation on the text (the title) of the main page of one of the websites that moves when getting on the page.
